Conflict

I've Got Hurt Feelings

July 20, 2015

This week's cultural blog post subject is conflict which is leaving me feeling conflicted. Dealing with conflict has not been a real issue for me as of late, so I'm not sure I have any great advice to expend on the subject. They'd like us to talk about a time when we experienced conflict in order to draw conclusions and summarize what we learned from the experience, but I believe conflict is a a bit too general a subject to use one cure-all method for all types of confict that arise in life.

I think, really, conflict in the workplace will boil down to consideration. When trying to get a job done, some people want to do things there way and aren't considerate of other people's time and value, like in the story about the dirty kitchen at DBC:SF. I consider myself to be pretty considerate, and I like to think I wouldn't direspect someone else in that way. As long as people are treating eachother like teammates and peers, I think conflict shouldn't be too big an issue.
